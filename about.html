Insaniyat Foundation

Insaniyat Foundation is a grassroots social organization dedicated to promoting political and legal awareness, public health, social responsibility, and human dignity. The Foundation undertakes initiatives including health camps, cleanliness and sanitation drives, environmental protection, winter blanket distribution for the needy, and programs focused on women’s safety. Through awareness and service, Insaniyat Foundation works to strengthen humanity, justice, and social harmony.
